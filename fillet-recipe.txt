Ingredients:
 - 1/4 kg fillet
 - 3 cups bread crumbs
 - 3 table spoons olive oil
 - 5 table spoons turmeric
 - 1/2 tea spoon black pepper
 - 1/2 tea spoon chilli
 - 1 tea spoon garlic
 - Any other spices to suite your needs and taste

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine everything except the fillet and bread crumbs.
 	Make incisions on the fillet and fill them in with the mixture from before.
 
 3. In a large pan spread the bread crumbs. On top of that lay the fillet and
 	ensure that it gets completely covered. Cover the fillet with alluminium foil
 	and grill for 30 mins.
